Activity History ​

The Activity History is an audit log of your local actions within SwitchCraft.

Tracked Events ​

  • Analyses: Every file dropped into the Analyzer.
  • Packages Created: Every .intunewin or .intunemac generated.
  • Uploads: Every successful upload to Intune.
  • Scripts: Saved or generated scripts.

Functionality ​

  • Search: Find when you last worked on "Adobe Reader".
  • Retry: Click an entry to quickly reopen that file in the Analyzer or relevant view.
  • Export: Export the log to CSV for reporting purposes.
  • Clear: Wipe the history to free up local database space.
