# Home & Overview

The **Home View** is your starting point in SwitchCraft. It provides an at-a-glance summary of your environment and quick access to common tasks.

## Dashboard Widgets
*   **Recent Activity**: A timeline of the last apps you packaged, scripts uploaded, or analysis tasks performed. Click items to jump back to them.
*   **Intune Status**: (If connected) Shows the health of your tenant connection and sync status.
*   **Quick Actions**: Large buttons to jump immediately to:
    *   *Analyzer*: Start a new package.
    *   *Winget*: Find a new app.
    *   *Wizard*: Start the guided flow.

## Notifications
The bell icon in the top-right collects application notifications (Update available, Upload complete, Error reports).

## Navigation
The sidebar on the left helps you navigate between "Apps & Devices", "Tools", and "System" management. You can collapse it for more screen space.
